Noor Lima Boudakian

Noor Lima Boudakian holds an MA in Economics with a focus on the political economy of gender and empire from the New School for Social Research, where she also completed a BA in Economics and Global Studies.

Her research and teaching are on the impact of war on women's bodies, small-scale economic development through a gendered lens, and qualitative economic methodology. She has also worked as a translator since 2018.

Noor has lived, studied, and worked in New York and Yerevan and enjoys reading, boxing, cooking, and hiking.
